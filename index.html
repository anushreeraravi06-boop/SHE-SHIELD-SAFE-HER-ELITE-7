Personal Safety & Self-Defense Tips

  1. Stay alert and avoid distractions like headphones.
  2. Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, leave.
  3. Walk confidently and maintain eye contact.
  4. Avoid isolated or poorly lit areas.
  5. Keep emergency numbers on speed dial.
  6. Target vulnerable areas: eyes, nose, throat, groin.
  7. Use loud voice to attract attention.
  8. Carry personal safety tools if legal.
  9. Share live location with trusted contacts.
  10. Change routines regularly.
  11. Stay near crowds when feeling unsafe.
  12. Lock doors and windows properly.
  13. Learn basic self-defense techniques.
  14. Do not reveal personal information to strangers.
  15. Use She-Shield SOS immediately when in danger.
